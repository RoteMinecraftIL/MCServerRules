Rules

The rules are marked with the severity of the "crime" like this: [x] is severity x. [x++] is the severity increasing each crime time.

  1. Don't grief [1++]
  2. Don't cheat! The cheaters will be banned! [5++]
  3. Don't spam chat/ask for admin/etc. [2++]
  4. Keep a nice family-friendly chat for eveyone to have fun with. [2, then 3]
  5. No offensive skins, names or builds. [4++]
  6. Have fun!

Punishments:

Severity 1:

Warning. The warning is something like Don't [crime] again! Next time you'll be muted for 10 Hours!.

Severity 2:

Mute for 10 hours.

Severity 3:

Permanent mute.

Severity 4:

Kick.

Severity 5:

Ban for 1 week.

Severity 6:

Permanent ban from the server. No coming back until the next server reset, at December 25-31 2017.
